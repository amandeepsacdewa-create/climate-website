Technology & Climate Solutions

Technology plays a key role in tackling climate change by providing innovative and sustainable solutions to reduce environmental impact.

On this page

  • Renewable Energy
  • Smart Cities
  • Electric Transport
  • Carbon Capture

Renewable Energy

Renewable energy is one of the most effective ways to reduce climate change. Sources such as solar and wind energy do not produce harmful carbon emissions like fossil fuels.

Solar panels convert sunlight into electricity, while wind turbines use wind power to generate energy. These technologies are becoming more affordable and widely used across the world.

Smart Cities

Smart cities use advanced technology to improve efficiency and reduce environmental impact. This includes energy-efficient buildings, smart traffic systems, and improved waste management.

For example, automated lighting systems reduce electricity usage, and smart transport systems help reduce traffic congestion and emissions in urban areas.

Electric Transport

Electric vehicles are a cleaner alternative to petrol and diesel cars. They produce little to no emissions, helping to reduce air pollution and greenhouse gases.

Electric buses and trains also contribute to more sustainable transport systems, especially in large cities where emissions are high.

Carbon Capture

Carbon capture technology removes carbon dioxide from the atmosphere and stores it safely underground. This helps reduce the amount of greenhouse gases contributing to global warming.

Although still developing, this technology has the potential to significantly reduce emissions from industries and power plants in the future.
